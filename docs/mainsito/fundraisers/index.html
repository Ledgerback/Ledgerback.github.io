    Fundraisers #

    The LDCRC is currently running fundraisers for:

    1. Fellowships: Fund a researcher to engage in independent research to help further our mission without perverse incentives
    2. Conference: Help us raise funds to run a conference covering a topic on emerging technologies, organizational forms, human-compatibel futures, independnet research, or another topic of interest, that can help connect the public with our members and other persons of interest
    3. Cloud Infrastructure: Help us pay for our cloud infrastructure costs so that we can provide more resources to our members to conduct their research
    4. Honors & Awards: Help us recognize individuals and organizations producing and sharing knowledge that helps us have a better understanding of the future

    Additionally, the LDCRC plans to crowdfund for its projects. If you are interested, please consider donating to a project as well.
