Cooperating

The DCRC is open to all sorts of cooperation with any changemakers. Feel free to contact us and let us link your organization with the DCRC. We do not see ourselves as competitors to other actors, rather we want to collaborate with others to network the transformation movement. Once we determine we can cooperate, we will develop an agreement for a project detalining how we will operate together.
