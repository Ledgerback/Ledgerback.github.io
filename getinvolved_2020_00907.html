Get Involved

You can get involved with the LedgerbackØDCRC by participating in our crowdsourcing, social, research, and survey and polling activities, or joining our community as an advisor, helping us recruit new members, volunteer, partner, or member of a collaborative or network.

If you have any suggestions for us on how you want to get involved, please send us a message.

More Information

For more information, please refer to our resources.

Our educational materials cater towards learners who are self-motivated to study, diagnose and correct problems in the network society that are caused by or can be remediated by technology and alternative models.

We want to help these learners by developing materials so that they can critically analyze the world around them and look for the paradigm shifts in their thinking and models to help push towards a world of ledgerbacked systems.
