About the LedgerbackØDCRC

LedgerbackØDCRC is a member-owned, self-directed research and education center for fostering collaboration between stakeholders and unifying the study of the internet and society (i.e., the network society). The LedgerbackØDCRC primarily conducts basic research on the internet and society.

Vision

LedgerbackØDCRC seeks a fairer, ethical, more democratic world that is supported by ledgerbacked systems and thinking.

Mission

The LedgerbackØDCRC is a hub for fostering collaboration between stakeholders and unifying the study of the internet and society.
