Welcome to the LedgerbackØDCRC

LedgerbackØDCRC is a member-owned, self-directed research and education center for fostering collaboration between stakeholders and unifying the study of the internet and society (i.e., the network society).

Education

We develop and provide open educational materials that provide background to our research and cover themes in the internet and society.

Research

At the LedgerbackØDCRC, we seek to unify the study of the internet and society by bringing scholars together from a wide range of disciplines to conduct interdiscplinary research so we can understand how science, technology and society interact with each other.

Get involved

There are many ways you can get involved with the work going on at the LedgerbackØDCRC such as volunteering, taking a survey or poll, or even reading with us. For more ways to get involved, please refer to our resources.

Projects

We conduct various projects at the to further our understanding of the interent and society. For our ongoing projects, please refer to our resources.

Membership

As a cooperative, members are our lifeblood and our rasion d'etre, and that our members are self-defined and self-motivated enough to self-manage their work and how to collaborate with each other. Apply today and work with us to develop the knowledge and skills to advance the transition towards ledgerbacked systems and thinking. In exchange for participating in the cooperative's core and administrative activities and work towards its mission, we offer our members governance rights, access to our private library, member-only services, and more. For more information, please refer to our resources.

Services

If you need help with your research or just have an inquiry or question about the internet and society, the LedgerbackØDCRC is here for you. We offer research commission, problem statement identification, and strategy services to anyone in the public who needs them. For more information about our services, please refer to our resources.
