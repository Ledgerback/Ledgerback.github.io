About

Ledgerback Commons Research Cooperative (LCRC) is a commons-driven, member-owned and -operated knowledge cooperative for academicians, students, scientists, researchers, publishers, technologists, institutions, businesses, and writers geared towards the production, curation, storage, discussion and dissemination of knowledge on collective action and commoning.

Research

To better understand these contexts, we combine social and technical research with free and open source software (FOSS) and other technologies to foster collective action and commoning. We take a commons-based approach to our research, such that we collaborate with each other in developing research, and ensure that the frutis of our research are held in communal property. Our research is conducted in six research programs:

  1. Knowledge Commons
  2. Solidarity Project
  3. Open Finance
  4. Platform Cooperativism
  5. Cryptoeconomics
  6. Emerging Technologies

Partnerships

The LCRC is always interested in working with other individuals and organizations who are radically thinking about the future of collective action and commoning. If you are working on a project that covers collective action and commoning in one of our described contexts, please do not hesitate to contact us for assistance or collaboration.

Publications

We publish original research, guides, toolkits, courses, commentaries, prototypes, and other materials on collective action and commoning in the above contexts. Most recently, our publications have covered blockchain applications in the music industry. For more information, please refer to our Resources.

Services

The LCRC, in addition to producing materials for the general public, also offers its services to private individuals and organizations. For more information about the LCRC's services, please refer to our Resources.
