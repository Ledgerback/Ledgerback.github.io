Knowledge and Problem-Solving

We combine social and technical research with free and open source software (FOSS) and other technologies to grow the knowledgebase in this space, to advise projects and individuals in this space to make better decisions, and to foster the growth of collective action towards more equitable and democratic social and economic outcomes.

What is Ledgerback Commons Research Cooperative?

Ledgerback Commons Research Cooperative is a commons-driven, member-owned and -operated knowledge cooperative for researchers, publishers, writers geared towards the production, curation, storage, discussion and dissemination of knowledge.

Learn More

Research Areas & Challenges

Our approach to research (i.e., knowledge production) is the production of original research, reviews, commentaries, perspectives, toolkits, analyses, compilations and datasets that cover decentralized and open thinking and systems (DOTS) topics.

Objectives

We achieve our objectives primarily through knowledge production as described above, and also the production of FOSS-related technologies for the benefit of our members and the general public. Additionally, we achieve our objectives by informing the general public and related communities about the happenings in the DOTS space through articles, media, and events.
